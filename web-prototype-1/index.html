About Me

Hi! I am Arezou. I am a freelance Flower Designer who lives in London. My passion lies within nature and I promote the use of as many recyclable and environmentally friendly products and methods as possible in my work. I love fine art and believe in the power of simplicity. My eye for colours, textures and details is excellent, producing original and memorable work. Trust me and make your dreams come true. It would be an honor to take any orders for floral arrangements for any events.
